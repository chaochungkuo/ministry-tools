Adding German scriptures to English documents

This application does the following things:

  • Recieve an English document in docx format.
  • Identify all the scripture references in the document.
  • Convert English references into German system.
  • Add the German scriptures under each paragraph with light blue color.
  • Export another docx file with al scriptures.
